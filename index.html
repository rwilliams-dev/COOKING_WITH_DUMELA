About Me

Chef Dumela · San Francisco Bay Area · International Cuisine · East African Specialist

Food has always been a bridge — between cultures, between people, and between the everyday and the extraordinary. As a San Francisco Bay Area–based chef, I bring a deep love for international cuisine to every dish I craft, with a special passion for the bold, soulful flavors of East African cooking.

East African cuisine is vibrant, communal, and deeply rooted in tradition — and sharing it with new audiences is one of the greatest joys of my work. Whether it's the warmth of a slow-cooked stew or the fragrant layers of coastal spices, these are flavors that tell a story, and I'd love to bring that story to your table.

My mission is simple: to make your life a little easier and a lot more delicious. Whether you need a personalized meal plan to fit your lifestyle, easy-to-follow recipes you can master at home, or a chef to take the stress out of your next event — I'm here for all of it.

Let's make something memorable together.

Karibuni Mezani, Tule Pamoja.
